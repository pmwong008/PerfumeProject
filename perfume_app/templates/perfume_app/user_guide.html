User Guide

First, click on "Member" at the top right corner, then select "Sign Up" to access the registration form. After filling in your personal details, click the "Submit" button at the bottom to complete the membership process.

After completing the registration, you will be automatically logged in. Now you will see an "ai" icon in the top right corner. Click on it to access a questionnaire. All questions are optional, but to find the most suitable perfume for you, we recommend answering as many as possible. Once finished, click the "Recommend Perfumes" button.

The AI will search for perfumes that match your selected answers and criteria. The results will be displayed on the screen. If no results appear, please try again starting from Step 2.

Please select the perfume(s) you would like to receive a sample of. Then, click "Get Sample" to proceed to the confirmation screen. Choose one of the 4 available package options, verify that your name and address are correct, and finally click "Confirm" to complete your request. We will send out your sample(s) within 2-3 days.
